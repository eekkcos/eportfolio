Introduction to OCBC:

OCBC (Oversea-Chinese Banking Corporation) is the longest-established Singapore bank, formed in 1932 after merging 3 local banks. OCBC is Southeast Asia's second-largest financial services group by assets, with one of the world's highest credit ratings. OCBC's excellence is also recognized through multiple awards, like:

  • Best Digital Bank in Singapore 2024 (Euromoney)
  • Best Mobile Trading Platform Singapore 2024 (Global Banking & Finance)
  • Best SME Bank in Asia-Pacific 2024 (Global Finance)

OCBC also has multiple subsidiaries:

  • Bank of Singapore - offering private banking services
  • Great Eastern Holdings - Singapore's and Malaysia's oldest and most established life insurance group
  • Lion Global Investors - leading asset management companies in SEA
